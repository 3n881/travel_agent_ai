## Travel Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Cochin (Kochi)
- Stay at OYO Hotel Prime Palace
- Explore Fort Kochi, the Chinese fishing nets, Paradesi Synagogue
- Dinner at Malabar Junction

### Day 2: Cochin to Munnar
- Visit Jew Town, Depart for Munnar
- Stay at Green Tea View

### Day 3: Munnar
- Visit Tea Museum, Trek at Anamudi, Relax at Attukal Waterfalls

### Day 4: Munnar to Alleppey
- Stay at Alleppey Premium Houseboats
- Houseboat cruise, Alappuzha Beach

### Day 5: Kumarakom
- Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Ayurvedic treatment
